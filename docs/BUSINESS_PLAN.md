# UNIT_01 // BUSINESS_PLAN v2.0

## 1. MISSION & IDENTITY
**UNIT_01** is a contemporary physical design protocol. We operate at the intersection of material science, speculative architecture, and high-density geometry. We do not manufacture "toys" or "decor." We engineer tactile "studies" in form and weight.

### Target Audience
- **Gen Z Cult**: Individuals deeply invested in anime, sci-fi, and technical design logic (e.g., Evangelion aesthetics, industrial brutalism).
- **The Modern Collector**: Those who appreciate the "Drop" culture over generic retail availability.

---

## 2. THE BRAND EXPERIENCE
The brand is experienced as a "Spaceship HUD" or "Mission Control." It is an immersive, technical layer overlaying physical art.

- **Visuals**: Lunar White (#F5F5F7) base, HUD Cyan (#00F2FF) accents. Scanlines, corner brackets, and telemetry readouts.
- **Copywriting**: "Material Logic." Grounded, precise, stripped of marketing fluff. Focus on tolerances, finishes, and density.

---

## 3. THE ALLOCATION MODEL (Drops)
We do not hold endless inventory. We release **Studies** or **Units** in discrete batches called **Allocations**.

- **Drop 01 (Origin)**: `VOID FIGURE 01` (PLA Composite, Matte Black)
- **Drop 02 (Logic)**: `NEUROFORM CUBE` (Heavy-Density Resin)

*Status Lexicon:* Items are either **[ LOCALIZED ]** (Available) or **[ DE-FRAGMENTED ]** (Sold Out).

---

## 4. PACKAGING & UNBOXING (The Zero-Point Layer)
Packaging must feel like recovering a precise technical instrument from a void space.
- Matte black, high-density cardboard enclosures.
- Foam-milled recesses exactly mapped to the unit geometry.
- Included spec-card: *UNIT_01 // STUDY 0X // MANUFACTURED 2026*.

---

## 5. REVENUE STRATEGY (High-Density Value)
Because the product is framed as an engineered study rather than mass-produced decor, pricing maps to perceived technical value.

- **Base Units**: ₹1,999 – ₹2,499
- **Complex Studies**: ₹2,999 – ₹4,999

By leaning into the "Spaceship HUD" aesthetic and the collector identity, UNIT_01 sidesteps competitors on Amazon, placing itself closer to high-end streetwear and independent design labs.
